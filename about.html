About AJ Smith

AJ is a passionate student and aspiring full-stack developer, currently advancing her expertise in HTML, CSS, JavaScript, and React. With a rich background in Management, Consumer & Small Business Banking, Supply Chain, Administration, and Logistics, she brings a unique perspective to web development. Her experience across multiple industries and platforms allows her to understand diverse user needs and craft adaptable, relevant solutions. This blend of technical skills and industry insight positions her to create applications that meet the demands of real-world environments.

AJ has developed interactive web projects, such as a Horoscope Web App and a personal portfolio, showcasing responsive design and dynamic features. With certifications in Agile and Accessibility, she values creating inclusive and efficient web solutions. Outside of tech, AJ loves exploring the outdoors with her dog, hiking, camping, swimming, and has a creative side that includes beading and mechanical tinkering. Her curiosity also extends to true crime and mystery stories.
